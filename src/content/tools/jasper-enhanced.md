## Overview

Jasper is an enterprise AI marketing platform designed for marketing teams to create on-brand content across all channels at scale.

## Key Features

- **Brand Voice**: Train AI on your brand's tone and style guidelines.
- **Campaign Builder**: Create multi-channel marketing campaigns.
- **Content Templates**: 50+ templates for different content types.
- **Team Collaboration**: Shared workspaces and approval workflows.
- **Analytics**: Track content performance and optimization.
- **API Access**: Integrate into your marketing stack.

## Pros & Cons

### Pros

- Enterprise-grade features
- Strong brand voice consistency
- Campaign-level workflow
- Good team collaboration

### Cons

- Expensive for small teams
- Steeper learning curve
- Less flexible than general AI tools
