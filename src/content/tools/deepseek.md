## Overview

DeepSeek is developed by DeepSeek AI, a Hangzhou-based research company known for state-of-the-art open-source models. DeepSeek-V3 and DeepSeek-R1 achieve GPT-4 class performance at dramatically lower cost.

## Key Features

- **DeepSeek-V3** - 671B MoE model, GPT-4 class general intelligence
- **DeepSeek-R1** - Reasoning-optimized, rivals OpenAI o1 on math and logic
- **Open Source** - Full model weights publicly available
- **128K Context** - Extended document processing
- **Affordable API** - Priced at about 5% of GPT-4
- **Code Generation** - 80+ programming languages

## Pros & Cons

**Pros:**
- State-of-the-art open-source models
- Extremely affordable (free app, cheap API)
- GPT-4 class coding and math performance
- 128K context window
- Active open-source community

**Cons:**
- Newer company, less enterprise track record
- Primarily text-focused (fewer multimodal features)
- Limited enterprise support/SLAs
- Chinese company, potential regulatory considerations
