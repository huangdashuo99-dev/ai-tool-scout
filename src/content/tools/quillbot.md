## Overview

QuillBot is an AI-powered writing tool focused on paraphrasing, grammar checking, and content enhancement. It's popular among students and professionals who need to improve their writing clarity.

## Key Features

- **Paraphraser**: Rewrite text in 7+ modes (Standard, Fluency, Formal, etc.).
- **Grammar Checker**: Detect and fix grammatical errors.
- **Summarizer**: Condense long articles into key points.
- **Translator**: Translate content between 30+ languages.
- **Citation Generator**: Create citations in multiple formats.
- **Co-Writer**: AI-assisted writing in a full editor.

## Pros & Cons

### Pros

- Excellent paraphrasing capabilities
- Multiple rewriting modes
- Affordable pricing
- Chrome extension and Word plugin

### Cons

- Free tier limited to 125 words in paraphraser
- Some modes only available in premium
- Less comprehensive than Grammarly for grammar
