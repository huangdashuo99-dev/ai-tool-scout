## Overview

CapCut is a free, AI-powered video editor by ByteDance that has become extremely popular for social media content creation.

## Key Features

- **Auto Captions**: AI-generated subtitles in multiple languages.
- **Background Removal**: Remove backgrounds without green screen.
- **AI Effects**: Trending effects and filters.
- **Templates**: Thousands of ready-to-use templates.
- **Text-to-Speech**: AI voiceovers for videos.
- **Multi-Platform**: Web, desktop, and mobile apps.

## Pros & Cons

### Pros

- Completely free with no watermarks
- Excellent mobile app
- Trendy templates and effects
- AI features built-in

### Cons

- Advanced features limited compared to paid editors
- ByteDance ownership raises privacy concerns
- Can be overwhelming for beginners
