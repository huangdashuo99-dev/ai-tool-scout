## Overview

Tome is an AI-native presentation platform that creates narrative-driven stories and documents using AI generation and DALL-E images.

## Key Features

- **AI Storytelling**: Generate complete narratives from text prompts.
- **DALL-E Integration**: AI-generated images for presentations.
- **Web Canvas**: Infinite scroll canvas for flexible layouts.
- **Embeds**: Rich media embedding support.
- **Analytics**: Track engagement and sharing.
- **Export**: PDF and link sharing.

## Pros & Cons

### Pros

- Unique storytelling approach
- High-quality AI generation
- Beautiful default designs
- Good collaboration features

### Cons

- Less traditional slide control
- AI credits limited on free tier
- Newer platform
