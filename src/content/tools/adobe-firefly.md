## Overview

Adobe Firefly is Adobe's family of generative AI models, designed specifically for safe commercial use. Trained on Adobe Stock, openly licensed content, and public domain images, Firefly provides AI image generation that businesses can use without copyright concerns.

Integrated across Adobe's Creative Cloud apps — Photoshop, Illustrator, Express, and more — Firefly brings AI capabilities directly into professional creative workflows.

## Key Features

- **Text to Image**: Generate high-quality images from text prompts with various styles and aspect ratios.
- **Generative Fill**: Add, remove, or replace objects in images using AI directly in Photoshop.
- **Generative Expand**: Extend image boundaries with AI-generated content that matches your scene.
- **Text Effects**: Create stunning text effects and typography with AI.
- **Vector Generation**: Generate scalable vector graphics for logos, icons, and illustrations.
- **Commercial Safety**: Trained only on licensed content, providing IP indemnification for enterprise.

## Pros & Cons

### Pros

- Commercially safe — no copyright concerns
- Seamless integration with Adobe apps
- High-quality, professional output
- IP indemnification for enterprise users
- Good style control and variation options

### Cons

- Fewer artistic styles than competitors
- Credit system can be confusing
- Best features require Creative Cloud subscription
- Less community content than open-source alternatives

## 概述

Adobe Firefly 是 Adobe 的生成式 AI 模型系列，专为安全商业使用而设计。基于 Adobe Stock、公开授权内容和公共领域图像训练，Firefly 提供企业可以使用而无需担心版权问题的 AI 图像生成。

集成在 Adobe 的 Creative Cloud 应用程序中 — Photoshop、Illustrator、Express 等 — Firefly 将 AI 功能直接带入专业创意工作流。

## 核心功能

- **文本转图像** — 从文本提示生成高质量图像，支持多种样式和纵横比
- **Generative Fill** — 直接在 Photoshop 中使用 AI 添加、移除或替换图像中的对象
- **Generative Expand** — 使用与场景匹配的 AI 生成内容扩展图像边界
- **文本效果** — 使用 AI 创建惊人的文本效果和排版
- **矢量生成** — 生成可缩放的矢量图形，用于徽标、图标和插图
- **商业安全** — 仅基于授权内容训练，为企业提供知识产权赔偿

## 优缺点

**优点：**
- 商业安全 — 无版权担忧
- 与 Adobe 应用无缝集成
- 高质量、专业的输出
- 为企业用户提供知识产权赔偿
- 良好的风格控制和变体选项

**缺点：**
- 艺术风格比竞争对手少
- 积分系统可能令人困惑
- 最佳功能需要 Creative Cloud 订阅
- 社区内容比开源替代品少
