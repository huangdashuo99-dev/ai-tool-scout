## Overview

Descript is a revolutionary audio and video editor that treats media like a text document. Edit by editing the transcript — delete words to remove them from the video.

## Key Features

- **Text-Based Editing**: Edit audio/video by editing the transcript.
- **Filler Word Removal**: Automatically remove ums, uhs, and other fillers.
- **Overdub**: AI voice cloning to fix or add words.
- **Screen Recording**: Built-in screen capture with webcam.
- **Transcription**: Fast, accurate transcription in 100+ languages.
- **Publishing**: Publish podcasts and videos directly from Descript.

## Pros & Cons

### Pros

- Revolutionary text-based editing
- Excellent filler word removal
- Built-in screen recording
- Good transcription quality

### Cons

- Steeper learning curve
- Higher price than basic editors
- Limited advanced video effects
