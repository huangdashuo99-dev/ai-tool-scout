## Overview

Luma AI combines 3D capture technology with Dream Machine, an AI video generation tool that creates videos from text and images.

## Key Features

- **Dream Machine**: Generate videos from text prompts and images.
- **NeRF Capture**: Create 3D models from phone photos.
- **3D Rendering**: Interactive 3D model viewing.
- **API Access**: Integrate 3D and video generation.
- **iOS App**: Capture and view 3D scenes on mobile.

## Pros & Cons

### Pros

- Unique 3D capture technology
- Dream Machine quality is good
- Free tier available
- Growing feature set

### Cons

- 3D features have learning curve
- Dream Machine still in beta
- Limited video length
