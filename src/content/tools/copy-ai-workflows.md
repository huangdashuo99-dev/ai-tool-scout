## Overview

Copy.ai Workflows is an AI-powered automation platform designed for go-to-market teams to automate repetitive tasks and scale operations.

## Key Features

- **Workflow Builder**: Create custom AI workflows with drag-and-drop.
- **2000+ Integrations**: Connect with CRM, sales, and marketing tools.
- **AI Agents**: Automated task execution.
- **Templates**: Pre-built workflows for common GTM tasks.
- **Team Collaboration**: Shared workflows and approval processes.

- Extensive integrations

- Scalable
