## Overview

D-ID is an AI platform that animates faces in photos to create realistic talking head videos.

## Key Features

- **Photo Animation**: Bring any photo to life with talking head videos.
- **Voice Cloning**: Clone voices for avatar speech.
- **Text-to-Speech**: Multiple languages and voices.
- **API Access**: Integrate face animation into your apps.
- **Presenters**: Choose from diverse AI presenters.

## Pros & Cons

### Pros

- Unique face animation technology
- Easy to use
- Good API
- Affordable starting price

### Cons

- Limited to face animation
- Quality varies with input photos
- Watermark on free tier
