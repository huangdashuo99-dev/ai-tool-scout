## Overview

Baichuan is the AI assistant from Baichuan AI, founded by Wang Xiaochuan (former Sogou CEO). It specializes in vertical AI for healthcare and legal domains, with models ranging from 7B to 100B+ parameters.

## Key Features

- **Vertical AI** - Healthcare, legal, and financial domain models
- **Large Model Portfolio** - 7B to 100B+ parameters
- **Medical Knowledge** - Trained on medical literature and clinical data
- **Legal Expertise** - Contract analysis, legal research, compliance
- **Open Source** - Multiple models released as open source
