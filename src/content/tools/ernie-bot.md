## Overview

ERNIE Bot (Wenxin Yiyan) is Baidu's flagship AI assistant, powered by the ERNIE large language model. Launched in March 2023, it has become one of China's most widely used AI chatbots with over 100 million users.

## Key Features

- **Advanced Chinese NLP** - Superior understanding of Chinese language, classical literature, and cultural context
- **Multimodal** - Text, image generation, document analysis, and voice interaction
- **Baidu Ecosystem** - Integrated with Baidu Search, Maps, and other services
- **Enterprise Solutions** - Custom model fine-tuning and private deployment

## Pros & Cons

**Pros:**
- Best-in-class Chinese language understanding
- Strong multimodal capabilities
- Deep Baidu ecosystem integration
- Robust enterprise deployment options

**Cons:**
- Limited English performance vs GPT-4
- Requires Chinese phone number for full access
- Smaller international community
