## Overview

DALL-E 3 is OpenAI's latest image generation model, representing a significant leap forward in AI-generated imagery. What sets DALL-E 3 apart from competitors is its exceptional ability to follow complex text prompts accurately and its groundbreaking capability to render legible text within images — something previous AI image generators struggled with.

DALL-E 3 is integrated directly into ChatGPT, making it incredibly accessible. Rather than requiring a separate platform or interface, you can generate images through natural conversation with ChatGPT, which then refines and generates the image based on your dialogue. This conversational approach to image creation makes it intuitive for users of all skill levels.

## Key Features

- **Superior Prompt Adherence**: DALL-E 3 understands and follows complex, detailed prompts with remarkable accuracy — generating images that closely match your description.
- **In-Image Text Rendering**: Accurately render legible text within generated images — ideal for posters, logos, social media graphics, and marketing materials.
- **ChatGPT Integration**: Generate images through natural conversation in ChatGPT, with the ability to iterate and refine through dialogue.
- **Safety Built-In**: Refuses to generate images of public figures, violent content, or inappropriate material, with built-in content moderation.
- **Automatic Prompt Enhancement**: ChatGPT automatically expands and improves your prompts for better results before generating images.
- **Regional Availability**: Available in 180+ countries through ChatGPT, with API access for developers to integrate into applications.

## Pros & Cons

### Pros

- Best-in-class prompt adherence and text rendering
- Easy access through ChatGPT — no separate platform needed
- Free tier available with daily image generation limits
- Natural conversational interface for iterative image creation

### Cons

- Lower artistic quality compared to Midjourney for creative/artistic work
- Limited control over style, composition, and camera settings
- Content safety filters can be overly restrictive for legitimate use cases
- No API for image editing or inpainting (generation only)

## 概述

DALL-E 3 是 OpenAI 最新的图像生成模型，代表了 AI 生成图像的一次重大飞跃。DALL-E 3 区别于竞品的最大特点是其卓越地遵循复杂文本提示词的能力，以及在图像中渲染清晰文字的革命性突破 — 这是此前的 AI 图像生成器一直难以解决的问题。

DALL-E 3 直接集成在 ChatGPT 中，使其极易获取。你不需要额外的平台或界面，只需通过与 ChatGPT 的自然对话即可生成图像，ChatGPT 会根据你的对话内容来优化和生成图片。这种对话式的图像创作方式让各种技能水平的用户都能轻松上手。

## 核心功能

- **卓越的提示词遵循** — DALL-E 3 能以惊人的准确度理解和遵循复杂、详细的提示词，生成与你描述高度匹配的图像
- **图中文字渲染** — 在生成的图像中准确渲染清晰可读的文字 — 非常适合海报、标志、社交媒体图形和营销素材
- **ChatGPT 集成** — 通过与 ChatGPT 的自然对话生成图像，并可通过对话进行迭代优化
- **内置安全机制** — 拒绝生成公众人物、暴力内容或不当材料的图像，内置内容审核
- **自动提示词增强** — ChatGPT 会在生成图像前自动扩展和优化你的提示词以获得更好的效果
- **区域覆盖** — 通过 ChatGPT 在 180 多个国家/地区可用，同时为开发者提供 API 以集成到应用中

## 优缺点

**优点：**
- 业界最佳的提示词遵循度和文字渲染能力
- 通过 ChatGPT 轻松访问 — 无需独立平台
- 提供免费版，每天有图像生成额度
- 自然的对话界面，支持迭代式图像创作

**缺点：**
- 与 Midjourney 相比，创意/艺术作品的艺术质量较低
- 对风格、构图和镜头设置的操控有限
- 内容安全过滤对某些正当用例可能过于严格
- 没有图像编辑或修复的 API（仅支持生成）
