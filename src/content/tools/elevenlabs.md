## Overview

ElevenLabs is the industry leader in AI voice synthesis, offering ultra-realistic speech generation and voice cloning technology.

## Key Features

- **Text-to-Speech**: Generate natural-sounding speech in 29 languages.
- **Voice Cloning**: Clone any voice from a short audio sample.
- **Voice Library**: Access 1000+ pre-made voices.
- **Speech-to-Speech**: Transform your voice in real-time.
- **Sound Effects**: Generate custom sound effects from text.
- **API Access**: Integrate voice synthesis into your apps.
