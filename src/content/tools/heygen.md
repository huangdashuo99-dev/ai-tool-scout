## Overview

HeyGen is an AI video generation platform that creates professional videos with realistic digital avatars, voice cloning, and multi-language translation.

## Key Features

- **Digital Avatars**: Choose from 100+ avatars or create custom ones.
- **Voice Cloning**: Clone any voice for avatar speech.
- **Auto Translation**: Translate videos into 40+ languages.
- **Script to Video**: Generate videos from text scripts.
- **API Access**: Integrate video generation into your apps.

## Pros & Cons

### Pros

- Realistic avatar movements and lip-sync
- Good voice cloning quality
- Multi-language support
- Easy to use

### Cons

- Expensive for frequent use
- Limited free credits
- Avatar variety could be better
