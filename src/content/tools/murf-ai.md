## Overview

Murf AI is a professional voiceover platform that generates studio-quality voiceovers for various content types.

## Key Features

- **120+ Voices**: Diverse voices across 20+ languages.
- **Voice Cloning**: Create custom voices.
- **Video Integration**: Sync voiceovers with video timelines.
- **Pitch & Speed Control**: Fine-tune voice characteristics.
- **Collaboration**: Team workspace for shared projects.

## Pros & Cons

### Pros

- Professional voice quality
- Good language variety
- Video timeline integration
- Team collaboration features

### Cons

- No free tier
- Higher starting price
- Voice variety could be better
