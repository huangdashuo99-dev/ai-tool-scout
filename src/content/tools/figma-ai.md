## Overview

Figma AI brings artificial intelligence features directly into the industry-standard design tool, automating repetitive tasks and providing smart suggestions.

## Key Features

- **Auto Layout Suggestions**: AI recommends optimal layouts for your designs.
- **Content Generation**: Generate realistic placeholder content.
- **Rename Layers**: Automatically organize and name layers.
- **Design Recommendations**: Get suggestions for improving your designs.
- **AI-Powered Search**: Find components and designs faster.

## Pros & Cons

### Pros

- Integrated into existing Figma workflow
- Automates tedious design tasks
- Free to use with Figma
- Continuously improving

### Cons

- Less powerful than standalone AI tools
- AI features still maturing
- Limited generative capabilities
