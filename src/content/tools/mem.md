## Overview

Mem is an AI-powered note-taking app that automatically organizes your notes and surfaces relevant information using artificial intelligence.

## Key Features

- **Auto-Organization**: AI automatically links and organizes notes.
- **Smart Search**: Find notes with natural language queries.
- **Related Notes**: AI surfaces relevant notes as you write.
- **Quick Capture**: Fast note creation with minimal friction.
- **Import**: Bring in notes from other apps.
- **Templates**: Pre-built templates for common use cases.

## Pros & Pros

### Pros

- Automatic organization saves time
- Clean, minimal interface
- Good search capabilities
- Import from other apps

### Cons

- AI features require paid plan
- Less customizable than Notion
- Smaller community
