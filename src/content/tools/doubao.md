## Overview

Doubao is ByteDance's AI chatbot, launched in early 2024. It offers conversational AI, image generation via Jimeng AI, coding assistance, and document analysis. Integrated with Douyin, Feishu, and Jinri Toutiao, it's one of China's most accessible AI assistants with over 50 million users.

## Key Features

- **Douyin Integration** - AI within China's largest short-video platform
- **Multimodal** - Text, image, and video understanding
- **Feishu Integration** - Built-in AI for workplace collaboration
- **Mobile-First** - Optimized for smartphone with voice input

## Pros & Cons

**Pros:**
- Completely free
- Massive reach via ByteDance ecosystem
- Strong multimodal capabilities
- Excellent mobile experience

**Cons:**
- New product, evolving features
- Privacy concerns with ByteDance
- Limited enterprise/API offerings
