## Overview

Fireflies.ai is an AI meeting assistant that automatically records, transcribes, and analyzes voice conversations across multiple meeting platforms.

## Key Features

- **Auto-Join Meetings**: Bot joins automatically and records everything.
- **Unlimited Transcription**: Free tier includes unlimited transcription.
- **Topic Tracking**: AI identifies key topics discussed.
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Detects positive, negative, and neutral sentiments.
- **Action Items**: Extracts tasks and follow-ups automatically.
- **CRM Integration**: Syncs notes with Salesforce, HubSpot, etc.

## Pros & Cons

### Pros

- Unlimited transcription on free tier
- Multiple platform support
- Good analytics and insights
- CRM integration

### Cons

- Bot presence may feel intrusive
- Accuracy varies with audio quality
- Advanced features need paid plan
