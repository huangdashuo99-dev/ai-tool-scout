## Overview

Synthesia is an enterprise-grade AI video platform used by major companies for creating training, marketing, and communication videos with AI avatars.

## Key Features

- **150+ AI Avatars**: Professional and diverse avatar options.
- **120+ Languages**: Create videos in virtually any language.
- **Custom Avatars**: Create digital twins of real people.
- **Template Library**: Pre-built templates for common use cases.
- **Brand Kit**: Custom colors, logos, and fonts.
- **Analytics**: Track video engagement and performance.

## Pros & Cons

### Pros

- Enterprise-grade quality and reliability
- Extensive language support
- Custom avatar creation
- Strong brand customization

### Cons

- Expensive for individuals
- Limited creative flexibility
- Avatar movements can feel unnatural
