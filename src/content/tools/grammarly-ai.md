## Overview

Grammarly is one of the most established AI writing assistants, now enhanced with powerful generative AI capabilities. Originally known for its grammar and spell-checking, Grammarly has evolved into a comprehensive writing platform that helps with tone, clarity, delivery, and now AI-powered content generation.

With Grammarly's generative AI features, users can draft emails, outlines, and content from prompts, as well as use AI to revise and improve existing text. The platform works across virtually every writing surface through browser extensions, desktop apps, and native integrations — making it a universal writing companion.

Used by over 30 million people and 70,000 teams, Grammarly is particularly valuable for professionals, students, and non-native English speakers who want to ensure their writing is clear, correct, and appropriately toned.

## Key Features

- **Grammar & Spelling Correction**: Real-time detection and correction of grammar errors, spelling mistakes, and punctuation issues across all your writing.
- **Tone Detection**: AI analyzes your writing's tone (formal, confident, friendly, etc.) and suggests adjustments for your audience.
- **Clarity & Delivery Suggestions**: Get recommendations for improving sentence structure, word choice, and readability.
- **Generative AI (GrammarlyGO)**: Draft emails, documents, and responses from prompts, or use AI to revise and rewrite existing text.
- **Brand Tone & Style Guides**: (Business plan) Enforce consistent brand voice and writing standards across teams.
- **Cross-Platform Support**: Works in browsers, desktop apps, Microsoft Office, Google Docs, Slack, and via mobile keyboards.

## Pros & Cons

### Pros

- Generous free tier covering essential grammar and spelling
- Works everywhere you write via browser extension and integrations
- Excellent tone detection and adjustment suggestions
- Strong privacy and security practices

### Cons

- Premium features are relatively expensive at $12/month
- Advanced writing style suggestions can feel nitpicky
- Generative AI features are less capable than dedicated tools like ChatGPT
- Limited to English language writing assistance
- Some users find the frequency of suggestions distracting

## 概述

Grammarly 是最成熟的 AI 写作助手之一，现已增强了强大的生成式 AI 能力。Grammarly 最初以语法和拼写检查闻名，如今已发展为一个全面的写作平台，涵盖语气、清晰度、表达优化以及 AI 驱动的内容生成。

借助 Grammarly 的生成式 AI 功能，用户可以从提示词起草邮件、大纲和内容，也可以使用 AI 修改和改善现有文本。该平台通过浏览器扩展、桌面应用和原生集成几乎覆盖了所有写作场景 — 使其成为通用的写作伴侣。

超过 3000 万用户和 7 万个团队在使用 Grammarly，它对希望确保写作清晰、准确、语气得体的专业人士、学生和非英语母语者尤其有价值。

## 核心功能

- **语法与拼写纠正** — 实时检测和纠正你所有写作中的语法错误、拼写错误和标点问题
- **语气检测** — AI 分析你的写作语气（正式、自信、友好等），并针对受众提出调整建议
- **清晰度与表达建议** — 获得改善句子结构、用词选择和可读性的建议
- **生成式 AI（GrammarlyGO）** — 从提示词起草邮件、文档和回复，或使用 AI 修改和重写现有文本
- **品牌语调与风格指南** — （Business 方案）在团队中强制执行一致的品牌语调和写作标准
- **跨平台支持** — 在浏览器、桌面应用、Microsoft Office、Google Docs、Slack 中可用，并支持移动键盘

## 优缺点

**优点：**
- 慷慨的免费版覆盖基础语法和拼写
- 通过浏览器扩展和集成在你写作的任何地方都能使用
- 出色的语气检测和调整建议
- 强大的隐私和安全实践

**缺点：**
- Premium 功能相对较贵，$12/月
- 高级写作风格建议可能显得过于吹毛求疵
- 生成式 AI 功能不如 ChatGPT 等专用工具强大
- 仅限于英语写作辅助
- 部分用户觉得建议频率过高，容易造成干扰
