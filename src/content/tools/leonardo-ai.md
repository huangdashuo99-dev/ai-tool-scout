## Overview

Leonardo AI is an AI image generation platform that offers custom model training, fine-tuned models for various styles, and real-time canvas editing for creative professionals.

## Key Features

- **Custom Model Training**: Train AI models on your own style or brand.
- **Fine-Tuned Models**: Access 30+ pre-trained models for different art styles.
- **Real-Time Canvas**: Edit and refine images interactively.
- **Image-to-Image**: Transform existing images with AI.
- **Texture Generation**: Create seamless textures for 3D and game design.
- **Community Feed**: Browse and remix creations from other users.

## Pros & Cons

### Pros

- Custom model training is unique
- Good free tier with daily tokens
- Large variety of art styles
- Active community

### Cons

- Token-based system can be confusing
- Some features only in higher tiers
- Quality varies by model
