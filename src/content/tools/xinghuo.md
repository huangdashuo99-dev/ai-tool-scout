## Overview

iFlytek Spark is the AI assistant from iFlytek, China's leading AI speech technology company. Launched in May 2023, Spark leverages 25+ years of speech tech expertise for superior voice capabilities across education, healthcare, and legal sectors.

## Key Features

- **Voice Interaction** - Real-time voice with industry-leading ASR and TTS
- **Dialect Support** - Cantonese, Sichuanese, and other Chinese dialects
- **Educational AI** - Tutoring, essay grading, personalized learning
- **Multi-Platform** - Web, mobile, smart speakers, in-vehicle systems

## Pros & Cons

**Pros:**
- Unmatched voice and speech capabilities
- 25+ years of speech technology heritage
- Strong educational solutions
- Multi-dialect support

**Cons:**
- Text capabilities behind GPT-4 class
- English performance not as strong
- Fragmented cross-platform experience
