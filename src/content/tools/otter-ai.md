## Overview

Otter.ai is an AI-powered meeting transcription and note-taking tool that captures, transcribes, and summarizes meetings in real-time.

## Key Features

- **Real-Time Transcription**: Live transcription during meetings.
- **Meeting Summaries**: AI-generated summaries with action items.
- **Speaker Identification**: Automatically identifies different speakers.
- **Integration**: Works with Zoom, Google Meet, and Teams.
- **Searchable Archive**: Find any meeting moment instantly.
- **Collaboration**: Share notes and highlight key points.

## Pros & Cons

### Pros

- Excellent real-time transcription
- Good meeting integrations
- Action item extraction
- Searchable meeting archive

### Cons

- Accuracy varies with accents
- Free tier limited to 300 minutes
- English-focused
