## Overview

Perplexity is an AI-powered search engine that revolutionizes how you find information online. Instead of returning a list of links like traditional search engines, Perplexity synthesizes answers from multiple sources and provides citations for every claim, making it easy to verify accuracy and explore further.

With over 10 million monthly active users, Perplexity has become the go-to tool for researchers, professionals, and anyone who wants fast, reliable, and well-sourced information.

## Key Features

- **Cited Answers**: Every response includes numbered citations linking to source documents, so you can verify information instantly.
- **Real-time Search**: Searches the web in real-time for every query, ensuring current and up-to-date information.
- **Multi-source Synthesis**: Combines information from multiple authoritative sources into a single, coherent answer.
- **Follow-up Questions**: Ask clarifying questions to dive deeper into any topic without starting a new search.
- **File Upload**: Analyze PDFs, documents, and images with AI-powered insights.
- **Collections**: Save and organize searches into collections for ongoing research projects.

## Pros & Cons

### Pros

- Eliminates need to click through multiple search results
- All answers are sourced and cited
- Real-time web access ensures current information
- Clean, ad-free interface
- Excellent for research and fact-checking

### Cons

- Can occasionally misinterpret source material
- Free tier has daily Pro search limits
- Less useful for navigational queries (finding specific websites)
- Citations sometimes link to less authoritative sources

## 概述

Perplexity 是一个 AI 驱动的搜索引擎，彻底改变了你在线查找信息的方式。它不像传统搜索引擎那样返回链接列表，而是从多个来源综合答案，并为每个声明提供引用，使验证准确性和进一步探索变得轻而易举。

拥有超过 1000 万月活跃用户，Perplexity 已成为研究人员、专业人士以及任何需要快速、可靠、有来源信息的人的首选工具。

## 核心功能

- **带引用的答案** — 每个回答都包含编号引用，链接到源文档，可即时验证信息
- **实时搜索** — 为每个查询实时搜索网络，确保信息最新
- **多源综合** — 将多个权威来源的信息组合成单一、连贯的答案
- **追问** — 提出澄清问题以深入了解任何主题，无需开始新搜索
- **文件上传** — 使用 AI 驱动的洞察分析 PDF、文档和图像
- **Collections** — 将搜索保存并组织到集合中，用于持续研究项目

## 优缺点

**优点：**
- 无需点击多个搜索结果
- 所有答案都有来源和引用
- 实时网络访问确保信息最新
- 简洁、无广告的界面
- 非常适合研究和事实核查

**缺点：**
- 偶尔会误解原始材料
- 免费版每日 Pro 搜索次数有限
- 对于导航查询（查找特定网站）不太有用
- 有时引用链接到权威性较低的来源
