## Overview

Kimi is developed by Moonshot AI, a Beijing startup founded by former Google Brain and Tsinghua researchers. Launched in early 2024, it's known for exceptional long-context handling.

With a 2M token context window, Kimi can process entire books, legal documents, research papers, or complete codebases in one session.

## Key Features

- **2M Token Context** - One of the largest context windows available
- **Long Document Analysis** - Process entire books, contracts, and papers
- **Deep Research** - Multi-step research with internet access and citations
- **Code Understanding** - Analyze complete codebases and architectures
- **File Upload** - PDF, Word, Excel, PPT, images, and code

## Pros & Cons

**Pros:**
- Industry-leading context window
- Excellent long-form analysis

**Cons:**
