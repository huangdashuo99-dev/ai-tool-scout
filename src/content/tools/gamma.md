## Overview

Gamma is an AI-powered tool that creates beautiful presentations, documents, and websites from simple text prompts.

## Key Features

- **AI Generation**: Create complete presentations from a topic description.
- **Multiple Formats**: Presentations, documents, and web pages.
- **Custom Themes**: Branded templates and designs.
- **Interactive Elements**: Embed videos, charts, and apps.
- **Collaboration**: Real-time team editing.
- **Analytics**: Track viewer engagement.

## Pros & Cons

### Pros

- Fast, high-quality output
- Multiple format support
- Interactive embeds
- Easy sharing and analytics

### Cons

- Less design control than PowerPoint
- AI output needs refinement
- Limited offline access
